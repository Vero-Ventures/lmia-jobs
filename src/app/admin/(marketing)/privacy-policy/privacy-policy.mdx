# Privacy Policy

## Information Collection

Manage Opportunities records and retains the email address associated with your account for the duration of its existence. Any information provided during the process of creating a job posting is voluntary and included at your discretion. This information will be displayed across the Opportunity job boards.



## Payment Handling

Payments are handled through the third-party service 'Stripe.' No payment information is collected or stored by Manage Opportunities. For more details, please refer to the [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/en-ca/privacy).



## Managing Your Information

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and its associated information at any time. To begin the data removal process, please contact us at: __contact@manageopportunities.ca__



## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy as needed at our discretion. Any changes to the policy will include an "Update" section to record the date of the policy change.


## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: __contact@manageopportunities.ca__
