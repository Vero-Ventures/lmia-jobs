# About Us

## Our Mission

Finding the right talent while meeting Canadian LMIA requirements can be overwhelming.
That's why we created a platform that lets you manage all your job postings in one place while ensuring they reach the right audiences.
With a single post, you can advertise across multiple job boards, each dedicated to supporting specific communities: 
Asylum Seekers, Disabled individuals, Indigenous peoples, Youth, and Recent Immigrants.

## Who Are We?
